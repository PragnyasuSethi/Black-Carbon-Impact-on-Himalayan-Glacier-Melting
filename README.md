# Black-Carbon-Impact-on-Himalayan-Glacier-Melting

> **A silent climate threat with massive consequences**

Black carbon (BC), released from **fossil fuel and biomass combustion**, is significantly accelerating the melting of Himalayan glaciers through two key mechanisms:
-  **Atmospheric heating**
-  **Surface darkening (albedo reduction)**

---

###  Key Insights from AI Analysis

-  Analyzed **100,000+ environmental observations**
-  BC contributes to **~13% of annual glacier melting**
-  Peak impact observed during **March–April**
-  **Random Forest model** achieved **R² = 0.895**
-  **PM2.5 identified as the most influential predictor**

---

###  Why It Matters

-  Himalayan glaciers support water supply for **750+ million people**
-  Accelerated melting threatens:
  - Water security  
  - Agriculture  
  - Hydropower systems  

-  Highlights urgent need for **air pollution control & climate action**
